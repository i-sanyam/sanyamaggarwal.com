Nice guys never finish last

08 Nov 2021

If there was one life lesson that I could share, it would be: Nice guys never finish last. Your odds of getting lucky in a connected world like ours dramatically go up when you have people genuinely wishing you well.

People root for you when you are genuinely nice to everyone you interact with without any expectation. It doesn’t matter if it’s a teammate, customer or a stranger. It doesn’t matter if you are working for someone or running a business.
